About Merryland

Merryland High Schools are private boarding mixed O and A Level Secondary Schools, established on a firm Christian foundation and exceptionally Solid Education Excellence with the ambition to become the best Senior secondary Schools in Africa. Both campuses offer Sciences and Arts subjects, admitting students from all backgrounds within and outside Uganda. The main campus (Kigungu) was founded in 2001 and 2012 for Katabi Campus.

Kigungu campus sits on over 60 acres of land in Entebbe Municipality Division B, just 3Km after Entebbe International Airport at the shores of Lake Victoria. We praise God for the natural, conductive and ever green learning environments, with fresh lake Victoria breeze. Katabi campus sits on over 15 acres of land in Entebbe Municipality Division A, near the shores of Lake Victoria. The campus is located 2Km off Kampala Entebbe highway at Entebbe Mayor's Garden along Wava Busambaga road.

Curriculum Overview

Sciences

At Merryland High Schools, we Foster a deep understanding of the the sciences through, ands on experiments, engaging lesson, and real world applications. Our science programs cover subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, equipping students with analyticat skills, critical thinking, and a passion for discovery. Our well equipped laboratories and experienced teacher ensure that students gain practical experience alongside theoretical knowledge, preparing them for future careers in medicine, engineering, technology, and research.

Arts

Creativity thrives at Merryland High Schools through our dynamic arts program. Whether it is visual arts, music, dance and drama, we provide students with the opportunity to express themselves and develop their talents Our school encourages artistic exploration through exhibitions, performances, and competitions, nurturing students to become confident, innovative, and culturally aware individuals.

ICT

In today's digital world, ICT plays a crucial role in education and career development. At Merryland High Schools, we provide students with comprehensive ICT training, covering essential computer skills.Our computer labs are equipped with modern technology, enabling students to gain hands on experience in programming, digital literacy, and emerging technologies. Through ICT, we empower students to become innovative problem solvers and prepare them for careers in technology, business, and communication.

Games and Sports

At Merryland High Schools, we recognise the importance of physical activity in fostering a healthy and disciplined lifestyle. our sports program includes football, basketball, athletics, volleyball, and many other activities that promote teamwork, leadership and resilience. With well maintained sports facilities and professional coaching, we encourage students to participate in both competitive and recreational sports, ensuring a balanced and active school life
